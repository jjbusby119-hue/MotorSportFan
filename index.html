Introduction

Growing up, I always loved cars and car media. I was always fascinated by the speed and power of cars, and I loved watching car shows and movies. As I got older, I started to get more into motorsports, and I have been a fan ever since. Now I watch a whole lot of different types of motorsports including F1, F2, F1 Academy, WEC, ELMS, and the NLS

The Formulas

Formula 1 is seen as the top flight of racing. That does not mean that its the only interesting category. F2 can some times throw up some really crazy races and battles and its always fun to check out the all girls racing championship.

The Formula divisions are from lowest up. F4, F3, F2, F1. F1 Academy uses F4 cars.

Endurance

Endura is a whole different beast of racing. When you are fighting for a race win for 6 hours you need mental strength not just a fast lap.

In Endurance racing you share the car with your teammate meaning you will have to swap drivers mid race. this is to keep the drivers safe as driving flat out for hours on end alone can be dangerous.
